Skin Saving Smoothie

Everyone knows the general health benefits of smoothies and juices but how can they specifically help our skin? Below you will find a list of the best vitamins for your skin and in what foods they can be found. Making a smoothie with a combination of these ingredients can be a more fun way of getting in vitamins instead of taking supplements.

Ingredients

Iodine
Benefits: aids healing scars, encourages detoxification, regulates skins moisture levels
Found in: Prunes & dairy products

Zinc
Benefits: reduce appearance of scars, anti-inflammatory, relieves redness and irritation
Found in: Seeds (hemp, pumpkin, sesame), dairy products, cashew nuts, kale, dark chocolate 70-85%

Biotin
Benefits: reduces inflammation
Found in: Almonds, spinach, cauliflower

Niacin (B3)
Benefits: helps protect skin cells from sun damage
Found in: Avocado, milk

Vitamin C
Benefits: antioxidant, boost collagen, treats UV exposure, boost luminosity, smooths skin tone, anti-aging
Found in: Guavas, blackcurrants, kale, kiwis, broccoli, lemons, lychees, strawberries, oranges

Vitamin A
Benefits: supports skins immune system, strengthens skins outer layer, promotes collagen
Found in: Goji berries, kale, carrots, spinach
