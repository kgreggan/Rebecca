Review: Carbon Theory Cleansing Bar

This £6 face wash has hundreds of 5-star reviews but is it really worth the hype?

This cleansing bar uses organic charcoal to remove bacteria, excess oil and toxins and organic tea tree oil to reduce inflammation. Shea butter is also used to nourish and hydrate. Altogether these ingredients have been dermatologically proven to prevent breakouts, keeping skin clear and hydrated.

Personally, I have mixed feelings about this soap. It hasn’t made my skin any worse but it also hasn’t made it much better. For the price however I can’t really complain but I would prefer to pay a little more for something I would see better results with.
