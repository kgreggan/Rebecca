Vitamin A - Acnes Unsung Hero!

The skincare saviour you didn't know you needed.

Vitamin A (aka Retinol) has several positive health benefits for the skin, especially acne prone skin. The skin is a retinoid-responsive organ making it able to readily absorb Vitamin A when applied topically. Vitamin A supports the effects of the skins immune system helping to prevent the appearance of breakouts. A deficiency however can cause follicular hyperkeratosis, a condition caused by too much keratin in the hair follicles, resulting in raised papules (under the skin spots with no puss inside). Vitamin A also promotes the effects of the skins natural moisturising meaning whilst it is helping to heal acne it is also preventing the skin from becoming dry which a lot of other acne fighting ingredients do. Plus, it stimulates the production of natural protein to keep skin looking overall healthier, perfect for any areas of your skin that might be acne free.
